Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you.

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

At INCANTO, we prioritize your privacy and believe data belongs to you. We collect minimal information:

  • Anonymous Preferences: Occasion, budget, recipient relationship, personality traits, and interests provided in the Gift Finder are processed transiently for recommendations.
  • Optional Account Data: If you create an account, we store your email, username, and hashed password for authentication. We never sell or share this data.
  • Local Storage: Your favorites and recently viewed gifts are stored only in your browser's local storage — not on our servers.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies, analytics tracking, or third-party trackers on INCANTO.

2. How We Use Your Data

Preference Data: Your gift preferences are processed securely and only used to generate recommendations. We never use your data for machine learning, profile building, or behavioral analysis beyond the immediate recommendation request.

Account Data: If authenticated, we use your email and preferences to personalize your experience and save your cart and recently viewed items. Your password is hashed with industry-standard protocols (PBKDF2-SHA512).

Local Storage: Your "❤️ Saved Gifts" and "Recently Viewed" history are stored entirely on your device and never transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly save preferences to your profile.

3. Affiliate Links and Third Parties

INCANTO is a discovery engine, not a marketplace. When you click "Buy Now" for a gift, you are securely redirected to trusted third-party merchants (such as Daraz, Amazon, or local retailers).

Important: Once you leave INCANTO, you are subject to their privacy policies, not ours. We are not responsible for their data practices. We do not share your personal information with these merchants unless you explicitly provide it during checkout.

Affiliate Compensation: INCANTO may earn a small affiliate commission if you complete a purchase through our links. This does not affect the price you pay — it remains identical.

4. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS/TLS Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted.
  • Password Hashing: Passwords are hashed using PBKDF2 with a 120,000 iteration count — we never store plain-text passwords.
  • Database Security: Your data is stored in Supabase with row-level security, encryption at rest, and automated backups.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information to advertisers or data brokers.

While we maintain strict security practices, no system is 100% immune to breaches. We commit to notifying you within 48 hours if your data is compromised.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Access Your Data: You can download a copy of your account data by requesting it at privacy@incanto.ai.

Delete Your Account: You can request permanent deletion of your account and associated data. We will comply within 30 days.

Opt-Out: You can clear your browser's local storage at any time to remove saved favorites and recently viewed items.

Email Communications: If we send you emails, you can unsubscribe using the link in the email.

6. Revisions to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy notice to align with legal requirements or service improvements. Changes will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of INCANTO after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Contact & Data Requests

For privacy concerns, data access requests, deletion requests, or general inquiries:

Email: privacy@incanto.ai

We commit to responding to all legitimate privacy requests within 10 business days.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI choose gifts?

Our engine maps the personality traits and interests you provide directly to our curated database of 12,000+ items using semantic matching.

Are the gifts physically held by INCANTO?

No, we are a discovery engine. When you click "Buy Now," you are securely redirected to trusted affiliate partners like Daraz to complete the purchase.

Is my data saved?

Your quiz inputs are processed transiently and are not stored against a personal profile. Your favorites are saved strictly on your local browser storage.

Do I pay extra using INCANTO?

Absolutely not! INCANTO is 100% free to use. We may earn a small commission from merchants if you complete a purchase, but the price remains exactly the same for you.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times depend entirely on the third-party platform (like Daraz or local merchants) fulfilling your order. You can check the estimated delivery times on their respective product pages.
