Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you.

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

At INCANTO, we prioritize your privacy. We intentionally collect limited information. The primary data we collect is anonymous: the inputs you provide in the Gift Finder (such as occasion, budget, recipient relationship, and personality traits) to generate gift recommendations. We do not store this data assigned to a personal profile.

2. How We Use Informational Data

The information processed via the AI prompt stays strictly transient and is solely used to query our recommendation engine. Your "❤️ Saved Gifts" and "Recently Viewed" history are stored entirely locally on your device via localStorage.

3. Affiliate Links and Third Parties

When you click on "Buy Now" for a recommended gift, you will be redirected to third-party seller platforms (such as Daraz or independent merchants). These third-party sites have their own separate and independent privacy policies. We have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites.

4. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your usage data. However, remember that no absolute transmission over the internet or electronic storage method can fully guarantee security.

5. Revisions to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy notice from time to time to align with legal requirements or service capabilities. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of the policy.

6. Contacting Us

If there are any questions regarding this privacy policy or to request a data audit, you may contact us using the information below:

Email: privacy@incanto.ai

Support Center

Have a question? We've got answers.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI choose gifts?

Our engine maps the personality traits and interests you provide directly to our curated database of 12,000+ items using semantic matching.

Are the gifts physically held by INCANTO?

No, we are a discovery engine. When you click "Buy Now," you are securely redirected to trusted affiliate partners like Daraz to complete the purchase.

Is my data saved?

Your quiz inputs are processed transiently and are not stored against a personal profile. Your favorites are saved strictly on your local browser storage.

Do I pay extra using INCANTO?

Absolutely not! INCANTO is 100% free to use. We may earn a small commission from merchants if you complete a purchase, but the price remains exactly the same for you.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times depend entirely on the third-party platform (like Daraz or local merchants) fulfilling your order. You can check the estimated delivery times on their respective product pages.
